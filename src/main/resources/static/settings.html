📖 App Usage Guide

Getting Started

  1. Register your account with valid email and phone number
  2. Add emergency contacts who will receive alerts
  3. Enable location permissions for accurate tracking

Using the Panic Button

  1. Access your dashboard
  2. Click the large red "PANIC ALERT" button
  3. Alert is automatically sent to all emergency contacts
  4. Live location tracking begins immediately
  5. Cancel the alert if it was triggered accidentally

Reporting Unsafe Areas

  1. Navigate to "Report Unsafe Area"
  2. Click on the map to select location
  3. Provide description and risk level
  4. Submit for admin review

🔒 Privacy Policy

We take your privacy seriously. Your personal information and location data are encrypted and only shared with your designated emergency contacts during alerts.

  • We do not sell your personal data

  • You have full control over your emergency contacts
  • You can delete your account and data at any time
